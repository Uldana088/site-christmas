CHRISTMAS MOVIES

  • Home Alone (1990)

    Kevin, a young boy, is accidentally left home alone during Christmas. He creatively defends his house from two burglars.

  • Elf (2003)

    Buddy, a human raised by elves, travels to New York to find his real father. His cheerful and childlike nature spreads Christmas joy.

  • The Polar Express (2004)

    A boy boards a magical train to the North Pole, discovering the power of belief and the spirit of Christmas.

  • Intertwining love stories unfold during the Christmas season, highlighting romance, kindness, and the holiday spirit.

  • How the Grinch Stole Christmas (2000)

    The Grinch despises Christmas and tries to steal it, but his heart changes as he learns the true meaning of the holiday.

  • A Christmas Carol (2009)

    Ebenezer Scrooge, a greedy old man, is visited by three spirits on Christmas Eve, who show him the value of kindness and generosity.

  • The Holiday (2006)

    Two women swap homes during the holidays, leading to unexpected love and new beginnings.

  • National Lampoon’s Christmas Vacation (1989)

    Clark Griswold aims to create the perfect Christmas for his family, but everything hilariously goes wrong.

  • Miracle on 34th Street (1994)

    A man working as Santa Claus must prove in court that he is the real Santa, inspiring belief in the magic of Christmas.

  • Klaus (2019)

    A postman and a reclusive toymaker form a friendship that brings a town together, creating the origins of Christmas traditions.
